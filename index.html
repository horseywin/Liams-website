Meet Liam

Turning Ideas into Reality with Passion and Precision.

Hi, I’m Liam, a 13-year-old with a passion for creating incredible things from scratch. Whether it’s cardboard, metal, or plastic, I love turning raw materials into amazing projects. Armed with my trusty laser cutter and my brand-new 3D printer, I’ve brought ideas like Mandalorian armor and a miniature train scene to life. When I’m not crafting, I’m probably planning my next project or teaching others how to build!

What I Bring to the Table

Here’s what I specialize in

Cardboard Craftsmanship

With simple materials like cardboard, I create complex shapes and structures, proving that creativity can start with the basics.

Laser Cutting Expertise

I use my laser cutter to create precise, detailed designs in metal, wood, and acrylic. From armor plates to intricate model pieces, this tool takes my work to the next level.

Painting and Detailing

Attention to detail is everything. Whether it’s weathering Mandalorian armor or adding fine details to my miniature train scenes, I ensure every project looks authentic and polished.
